IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

In the mid 1930s, Colonel Harland D Sanders bought a motel and café in Corbin, Kentucky. Despite having tried his hand at several kinds of work – from railroading to operating a steam-powered ferryboat - Colonel Sanders loved to cook and was always experimenting with various combinations of seasonings.
